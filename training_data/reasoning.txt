1. If all dogs are animals and Rex is a dog, what is Rex?|Rex is an animal
2. If it rains, the ground gets wet. The ground is wet. Did it definitely rain?|Not necessarily — something else could have made the ground wet
3. A is taller than B, and B is taller than C. Who is the shortest?|C is the shortest
4. If no fish can fly and a salmon is a fish, can a salmon fly?|No, a salmon cannot fly
5. Book is to reading as fork is to what?|Eating
6. If all roses are flowers and some flowers fade quickly, do all roses fade quickly?|Not necessarily — only some flowers fade quickly, and roses may or may not be among them
7. If turning the key starts the car, and the car did not start, what can you conclude?|The key was not turned, or something else is preventing the car from starting
8. Monday comes before Tuesday, and Tuesday comes before Wednesday. What comes before Wednesday?|Monday and Tuesday both come before Wednesday
9. Pen is to writer as brush is to what?|Painter
10. If all cats have tails and Whiskers is a cat, what does Whiskers have?|Whiskers has a tail
11. A train leaves at 9 AM going 60 mph. Another leaves at 10 AM going 80 mph on the same route. Which will be ahead at 10 AM?|The first train, because the second just started
12. If some birds can swim and all penguins are birds, can penguins necessarily swim?|Not from this logic alone — we only know some birds can swim, not which ones
13. Eye is to seeing as ear is to what?|Hearing
14. If no mammals are cold-blooded and a whale is a mammal, is a whale cold-blooded?|No, a whale is not cold-blooded
15. If the battery is dead, the flashlight won't work. The flashlight isn't working. Is the battery definitely dead?|Not necessarily — there could be another reason the flashlight doesn't work
16. All squares are rectangles. Is a rectangle necessarily a square?|No — all squares are rectangles, but not all rectangles are squares
17. Seed is to tree as egg is to what?|Bird (or chicken)
18. If P implies Q, and Q is false, what can you conclude about P?|P must also be false (modus tollens)
19. Tom is older than Jane. Jane is older than Sam. Is Tom older than Sam?|Yes, Tom is older than Sam
20. If every student passed the test and Alex is a student, did Alex pass?|Yes, Alex passed the test
21. Ship is to ocean as airplane is to what?|Sky (or air)
22. If some apples are red and all red things are colorful, what can you conclude?|Some apples are colorful
23. There are five chairs in a row. Ben sits to the left of Amy. Amy sits in the middle. Where can Ben be?|Ben is in chair 1 or chair 2 (to the left of the middle chair 3)
24. If it snows, school is cancelled. School is not cancelled. Did it snow?|No, it did not snow
25. Glove is to hand as sock is to what?|Foot
26. All dogs bark. Rex does not bark. What can you conclude?|Rex is not a dog
27. If A is bigger than B, and C is bigger than A, which is the smallest?|B is the smallest
28. Water is to ice as milk is to what?|Frozen milk (or cheese, as a transformed state)
29. If eating too much causes a stomachache and John has a stomachache, did he eat too much?|Not necessarily — other things can cause a stomachache
30. All prime numbers greater than 2 are odd. 7 is a prime number greater than 2. Is 7 odd?|Yes, 7 is odd
31. Chapter is to book as episode is to what?|Series (or season)
32. If no reptiles have fur and a lizard is a reptile, does a lizard have fur?|No, a lizard does not have fur
33. Three of the four words belong together: apple, banana, carrot, grape. Which does not belong?|Carrot — the others are fruits, carrot is a vegetable
34. If studying leads to good grades and good grades lead to scholarships, what does studying lead to?|Studying leads to scholarships (transitive reasoning)
35. Helmet is to head as shield is to what?|Body
36. If all managers attend the meeting and Sara did not attend, what can you conclude?|Sara is not a manager
37. X is heavier than Y. Z is lighter than Y. Rank them from heaviest to lightest.|X, Y, Z
38. If cutting down forests reduces oxygen and reduced oxygen harms health, what does cutting down forests do?|It harms health
39. Steering wheel is to car as handlebars are to what?|Bicycle
40. If only members can enter the club and Dave entered the club, what is Dave?|Dave is a member
41. A factory produces 100 widgets per hour. How many does it produce in 30 minutes?|50 widgets
42. If all metals conduct electricity and copper is a metal, does copper conduct electricity?|Yes, copper conducts electricity
43. Thermometer is to temperature as clock is to what?|Time
44. If some teachers are strict and Mr. Lee is a teacher, is Mr. Lee strict?|Not necessarily — only some teachers are strict
45. If A causes B, and B causes C, and C is observed, did A definitely occur?|Not necessarily — C could have been caused by something other than B
46. Scales are to fish as feathers are to what?|Birds
47. If nothing illegal is permitted and jaywalking is illegal, is jaywalking permitted?|No, jaywalking is not permitted
48. A snail moves slower than a rabbit. A rabbit moves slower than a cheetah. What moves fastest?|The cheetah
49. If lack of sleep causes poor focus and poor focus causes mistakes, what does lack of sleep cause?|Mistakes
50. Keyboard is to typing as microphone is to what?|Speaking (or recording)
